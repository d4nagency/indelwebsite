Indelible was born and molded in the shadows during a time when tattoos were condemned, branding those who dared as criminals, degenerates, and outlaws.

In retaliation, we emerged as the first of our kind in fashion and tattoo culture. The values of Indelible took a back seat to the primal urge of permanently etching art into one’s own skin. Whether collaborating with the world's top brands or releasing our own exclusives, holders will forever be at the center of this world.
